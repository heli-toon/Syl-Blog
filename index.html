Decision tactics

  • Owusu Sylvia
  • Mar 1, 2023
  • Other

Wouldn't still apologize for hurting my feelings. Glances are exchanged as we pass by each other as if we were a pair of like poles which repel each other. Can a connection be felt? I ask myself? I pretend as if there was nothing to a line. Is it love or nothing? Eyes seems never to meet, pain and regret swallows me and it feels like am gasping for breath. Even desert do cry, all my regret are entombed from birth in earth. My tears lie hid from the glare of sight. Conscience, save me from the wrath of unaided humans. Dreams can't even forget your then existence, having not seen you in a day is like a stream gushing into the bowels of the oceans so silently to the extent that flies of the sky hardly find. Dreams do not lie! Your waywardness saddens me the most like a sculptured bride and I am not tough enough for all these. I act like a fastened rock facing nothing rolling to the end until I find my rightful owner.

Your memory is ecstasy. These words would forever remain in my heart. I weep inwardly like a madman chasing nothing. Trying to understand what the moment actually brings. Here I lie, reminiscing the moments we both embraced.

"Is it worth the wait? Counting on the universe to bring me what truly is mine? “Mine?” I ask myself. What truly is mine?"

Getting drowned in the moment. Thinking you would forever stay. Here, I find you lurching behind. Being paranoid with countless flashbacks which keep me thinking. And I ask myself once again, Is it worth the wait? The heart is fragile. Trust is essential and love is necessary. Tossing and turning, thinking about you, your actions and the void which has been created. Your absence makes me doubt is love is eternal. We were once bounded, fate brought us together and we tried to sustain it. Would you ever run back? Would I accept you back? Are we willing to forgive each other? Are we willing to fight once more? The wanderer who was once obsessed now feels at ease.

Light at the end of the tunnel

Embraced by the saying time is money. Gone were the days when apology was just an ordinary norm. Distance brought us apart the more. Reaching out for you when I needed you the most was very suffocating but as tables turned I understood what it actually meant to have a shoulder to have a shoulder to lie on. Someone to fill the void in created in my heart. My heart aches as it longs for the true one. The true one? The loving, understanding and fun one. Someone who is not self-centered. Someone who cares and feels compassionate. We still search for the light at the end of the tunnel. The mimicking of society seems like a play but is unbearably painful conscience. I know you are always right and you have been with me from birth. Lead the way into the path that has light and sound mind.

Surviving The Odds.

Smiles mixed up with tears wrestles against each other. Help, Help my voice rants but can't be heard. Am I happy or sad? I ask myself, “Why did you suddenly appear after days rolled into weeks, weeks into months and months to a year as I longed for your love?” Just trying to figure out what the moment hold. Just can't forget the pains you caused. I am hurt, I am broken, and the wanderer who was once obsessed once feels at ease. No, it's not worth the wait! Memories fades as I search the light to my path through the tunnel. To find answers to what really is mine. The pain in me has reached an alarming height that surpasses the limits of the sky. Love, indeed abounds with pain, you just have to identify them. My fears heightens as you grew closer to seek solutions to what really was worth it. Your presence has brought in its trail a tedious wave, yelling to find a place to hide. Your whirling life has worth no specific cause. No answers found at the end of the tunnel. Your existence diminishes as the days drew near.
