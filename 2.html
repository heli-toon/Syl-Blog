Escaping the Odds

  • Owusu Sylvia
  • Mar 1, 2023
  • Other

Life comes with an experience for strength for the future ahead. Fear against fear with each ray of the sun, but when trust is lost and complacency reigns, a hand is extended to save and to free. To assure and give hope for the wildest days. That the fear and despair of the unfavorable past. Can be a thing of the past with the help of prayer. That's if the present will receive a wage fair enough for the toil. Alas one's decisions may determine one's days, to remain in the choices no matter how much one may struggle, and to be free from the chains of rejections and fear.

Patience waits till the latter's mirth has subsided. Rage, Rage against the dying of the light in the tunnel, intently gazing in to the distance.

"Ego sucks us down like the law of gravity"

I see the toxic vanity of ego at play every day and it never ceases to amaze me how often it wrecks promising creative endeavors. Obstacle is the way and when there is a wall, there is a way. Brimming with ambition, makes us vulnerable to this darker side of the psyche. Ego's sense of superiority and certainty that exceeds the bounds of confidence and talent. It's when the notion of ourselves and the world grows so inflated that it begins to distort the reality that surrounds us.

Ignorance is the enemy

Self-confidence becomes arrogance, assertiveness becomes obstinacy and self assurance becomes reckless abandon. As our island of knowledge goes so does the shore of ignorance.

One can't recognise opportunities or create them. If instead of seeing what is in front of us, we live inside our own fantasy.If you start beleiving in your greatness it is the death of your creativity. Ignorance tells us what we want to hear and when we want to hear it.
